Ultimate stress test with five actors performing unusual coordinated behaviors. In heavy rain at an intersection, chaos unfolds: an erratic red Mustang ahead slows to 5 m/s then swerves left, accelerates to 12 m/s, and swerves back. A black Nissan approaches wrong-way at 15 m/s. A white ambulance behind accelerates with sirens. A panicked pedestrian runs then freezes. A gray truck is stalled in the intersection. This creates the ultimate edge case scenario testing all aspects of autonomous vehicle response.
